Sustainability

The materials in our jackets are of the best quality, so that you can be sure they last, and your ecological footprints stays as low as possible. Every item bought in one of our stores can be returned for a new life.

Responsibility

When we make our jackets we carefully select every aspect of our production - from the sours of our fabrics to the rights of the workers making our jackets. So that you can be sure you make the responsible choice.

Quality

We believe a new piece of clothing should last a lifetime. Thats why our designs are simple and timeless. If your garment ever needs a little extra love and reaper bring it to your nearest store, and we'll sort it out for you!.

Where we started

Our journey might as well have been yours, starting from our living room, in a little town in Norway. Johannes and I found ourself packing for an adventure, going to hike through Jotunheimen, but finding our beloved rain coats no longer being so rain resistant as before. Not wanting to spend all our money on new equipment that did not last, we found ourself in a pickle. We needed a brand that was transparent and honest about their production and pricing. Thus became RainyDays.

Where we started

We are compassionate about fair working conditions throughout our production line. Thus we only collaborate with producers that we know puts good working environment as their highest priority. For us this does not only mean good salary and safe working conditions, but also sustainable production taking care of our planet for generations to come.
